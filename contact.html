Formula 1 from a fan's point of view


As you may all know, F1 is a hard sport to understand. Journalists and commentators often only talk about specifics, like small aerodynamic changes, that only experts and veteran fans in the motorsport would understand. Like many people, this complexity hindered my ability to enjoy the sport when I was just getting into F1 racing.
That's why I wanted to create this website that explains general F1 information, rather than assuming fans already know what they are.
